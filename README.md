## 📌 Why This Repository?

As a **Physics student**, this archive serves as a **practical complement to my degree**. My goal is to bridge the gap between abstract theoretical equations and the life-saving reality of modern medicine.

### The Importance of Radiation in Medicine
Radiation is a cornerstone of oncology and advanced diagnostics. It allows healthcare professionals to:
* **Cure:** Destroying cancer cells through high-precision radiotherapy.
* **Visualize:** Seeing inside the human body without surgery (X-Ray, CT, PET scans).
* **Protect:** Designing shielding and dosimetry protocols to ensure the safety of both patients and staff.

Beyond standard lecture notes, this project includes additional development materials:

1.  **Radiation Fundamentals:** Particle interaction, radioactive decay, and cross-section analysis.
